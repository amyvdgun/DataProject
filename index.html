About Stock Checker 5000

Investing is all that matters!

The first chart shows the relationship between the Earnings Per Share (EPS) and the Beta of a particular company. By hovering over the dots, the company name, the EPS and the beta are displayed. By zooming and clicking on a certain dot, the line graph and the candlestick chart are updated.

The line graph shows the close price movements per period.

The candlestick chart displays the high, low, open and close price per period.
